Open Source Projects

WhichCourse

A search interface to aggregate and display course reviews scraped from Carnegie Mellon's official faculty evaluation site in a manner that allows for easy course comparisons.

Tower Defense

A real-time strategy based game that pits players against waves of enemies that become increasingly difficult to stop. Gameplay includes 10 enemy waves and 4 towers with varying characteristics.

Error Analysis

A research project that explored the robustness of various binary number representations with regard to bitwise errors and soft errors from radiation.
