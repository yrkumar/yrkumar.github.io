About Me

Hi! My name is Yashas Kumar. I am currently working towards a BS in Computer Science (with an intended minor in Computational Finance) at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science.

I grew up in the Silicon Valley and graduated from Mission San Jose High School, where I founded the Make-A-Wish Club, participated in science competitions (like the Siemens Competition), and helped lead the debate team.

I've previously worked at UCLA's NanoCAD Lab and at CMU's Machine Learning Department. As of Summer 2013, I'm interning on the Data Services team at Banno and working on a few cool tech projects in my spare time.
